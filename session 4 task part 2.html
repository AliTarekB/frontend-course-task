Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick Dish perfect for any meal

    Preperation time

  • Total:Approx 10min

  • Preperation:5min

  • Cooking:5min

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese, herbs ,diced vegetables

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs

  2. Heat the pan

  3. Cook the Omelette

  4. Add the fillings (Optional)

  5. Serve and fold

  6. Enjoy
